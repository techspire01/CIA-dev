Our Mission

The Chinnavedampatti Industrial Association (CIA), established in 2019, is dedicated to advancing Coimbatore's industrial sector, particularly in Chinnavedampatti. We focus on empowering micro and small industries by facilitating access to government benefits and promoting sustainable growth.

Our initiatives encompass industrial development, skill development workshops, technological advancements, and fostering inter-industry collaborations. We connect members to development programs and trade opportunities while representing MSME interests with industry bodies and government agencies.

Our Story

Under the leadership of President Mr. N. DevaKumar, CIA represents various industrial sectors including robotics and automation, motors and pumps, corrugated box manufacturing, packaging solutions, kitchen equipment and food machinery, steel doors and safety lockers, printing machinery, CNC job works, metal and steel fabrication, and laser cutting and sheet bending.

The association is committed to community engagement through initiatives like implementing CCTV surveillance for enhanced security, organizing annual flag hoisting ceremonies, and participating in disaster relief efforts such as the Kerala Flood Fund arrangements. We help member companies become more resilient, innovative and ready for new markets.
